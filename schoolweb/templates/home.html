About Us

Welcome to Our School

THIRUVANGOOR HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL was established in 1926 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in PANTHALAYANI block of KOZHIKODE district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school.

The school has Private building. It has got 46 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has a separate room for Head master/Teacher and 24 girls toilet and it is functional. The school has a playground.
